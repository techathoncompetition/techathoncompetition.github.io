Techathon 2019

Judging Criteria




Innovationeering abstracts will be judged by a panel of experts.

Creativity, Novelty and Design

How novel the idea is? Has the design been taken into consideration? The innovation must be ingenious and novel in its area of application.

Originalty and Feasibility

The innovation should not, by any means, include copied or stolen work. Such applications will be disqualified immediately. Also the solution should be implementable as described.

Potential of Impact

How the solution benefit society and the scale of problem it solves, intensity of the solution and number of people catered from the solution directly or indirectly.

In case of any discrepancies, the decision of the Organizers or Judges will be final and binding on all.
